Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account on EduConnect? +
To create an account on EduConnect, click on the "Sign Up" button on the top right corner of the homepage. Fill in your personal details like name, email address, and password. Verify your email address through the confirmation link sent to your inbox, and your account will be ready to use.
How do I enroll in a course? +
To enroll in a course, browse through our course catalog and select the course you're interested in. Click on the "Enroll Now" button on the course page. If it's a paid course, you'll be directed to the payment page. For free courses, you can start learning immediately after enrollment.
How do I track my progress? +
You can track your progress through your personal dashboard. Log in to your account and navigate to "My Learning" or "Dashboard" section. Here, you'll find all your enrolled courses along with progress indicators, completed modules, and upcoming assignments.
How do I get a certificate? +
Certificates are awarded upon successful completion of courses. Once you've completed all required modules, assignments, and assessments with passing grades, your certificate will be automatically generated. You can download it from your "Certificates" section in your profile.
What payment methods are accepted? +
We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers. In some regions, we also support local payment options. All transactions are secure and encrypted.
